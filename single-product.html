Features

Powerful Performance and Suction

The Power-force Suction and Multi-Surface brushes will leave no dirt, debris, and pet hair escape combined with the finer Edge-Sweeper.

Advanced Auto Dirt Disposal

For up to every 60 days, the robot will empty itself and keep your cleaning hands-free in the Auto Dirt Disposal mode.

Customizable Experience

The app lets you enjoy personalized features to fit your needs including scheduling and extra cleaning recommendations based on your habits and high traffic areas in your home.

Intelligent Cleaning

On both hardwood and carpet, the robot navigates in neat rows with smart tracking sensors and adapts to cover hard-to-reach areas.

The Sensor-detect Technology lets it know where it can reach without getting stuck on furniture.

Pet Friendly

The Multi-Surface brushes easily picks up pet hair, while the Triple Pad Filter traps dog and cat allergens.

Smart Charging

The robot automatically returns to its charging dock when low in battery, and picks up where it left off after fully charging.
